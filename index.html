Our Mission

The most valuable marketplace is the one in your neighborhood.

QSpot Technologies was founded on a simple conviction: connecting people with the spaces and services around them shouldn't be complicated. Whether someone has a spot to share or is looking for one nearby, that connection should feel effortless and safe.

We build software that empowers individuals and small businesses to put their space and skills to work, while giving people a trusted way to discover local opportunities they'd never find otherwise.

Everything we make starts local, respects privacy, and is engineered to the same standard whether you're on iPhone or Android.

Our Approach

How we build.

A small set of principles shapes every product decision we make.

🛡️

Trust by design

Progressive identity verification and secure, protected payments are built into the foundation — not bolted on.

📍

Local first

We prioritize proximity and real-world connection over endless feeds. The best opportunity is often the closest one.

🔒

Privacy & security

Encrypted storage, least-privilege access, and a defence-in-depth mindset protect the people who use what we build.

⚙️

Cross-platform craft

One shared, modern codebase delivers a consistent, high-quality experience on both iOS and Android.
